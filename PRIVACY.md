# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 1, 2026**

RF Animation LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the OilPaint Copilot mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

---

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Paint Collection**: The paints you mark as owned, including any notes or custom properties you add
- **Medium Collection**: The mediums you mark as owned
- **Projects**: Your painting projects, including reference images, layer history, and progress photos
- **Recipes**: Color mixing recipes you save

### Data Collected Automatically
- **None**: We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage data automatically

---

## How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:
- Provide the App's functionality (color mixing, layer tracking, etc.)
- Sync your data across your devices via iCloud (if enabled)

We do **not**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Track your usage or behavior

---

## Data Storage & Sync

### Local Storage
All app data is stored locally on your device in your app's private documents directory.

### iCloud Sync
If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs to Apple's iCloud servers using CloudKit. This sync is:
- Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- Stored in your private iCloud container
- Accessible only to you and your devices

We do not have access to your iCloud data. Apple's iCloud terms and privacy policy apply to data stored in iCloud.

---

## In-App Purchases

When you make a purchase:
- The transaction is processed entirely by Apple
- We do not collect or store payment information
- We only receive confirmation of purchase status from Apple to unlock Premium features

---

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Apple iCloud/CloudKit**: For optional data sync between your devices
- **Apple StoreKit**: For in-app purchases

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

---

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device and in your iCloud account until you delete it. You can:
- Delete individual projects, paints, or recipes within the app
- Reset your paint/medium collection to defaults in Settings
- Delete the app to remove all local data
- Manage your iCloud data through iOS Settings

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.
