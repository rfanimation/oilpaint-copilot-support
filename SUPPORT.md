# OilPaint Copilot Support

## Getting Help

Thank you for using OilPaint Copilot! We're here to help you get the most out of the app.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: What is OilPaint Copilot?**
A: OilPaint Copilot is a studio companion app for oil painters. It helps you mix colors accurately using color science, track your painting layers with fat-over-lean guidance, and get notified when layers are ready for the next application.

**Q: Is the app free?**
A: The app is free to download and use with up to 6 paints in your collection. For unlimited paints, you can upgrade to Premium for a one-time purchase of $3.99.

**Q: Which devices are supported?**
A: OilPaint Copilot requires iOS 17.0 or later and works on iPhone and iPad.

---

### Paint Collection

**Q: How do I add paints to my collection?**
A: Go to the Paint Library tab, switch to "All Paints" mode, and tap on any paint to view its details. Then tap "Add to My Paints" to add it to your collection.

**Q: Why can't I add more than 6 paints?**
A: The free version is limited to 6 paints. Upgrade to Premium ($3.99 one-time) to add unlimited paints.

**Q: Can I add custom paints not in the database?**
A: Yes! In the Paint Library, tap the + button to create a custom paint with your own color values and properties.

**Q: What if a paint's color doesn't match my tube?**
A: You can override any paint's properties (color, opacity, drying speed) in the paint detail sheet. Your overrides are saved separately from the master database.

---

### Color Mixing

**Q: How does the color mixing algorithm work?**
A: We use a hybrid Kubelka-Munk algorithm that models how pigments actually absorb and scatter light. This produces realistic results, especially for complementary color mixes that should produce browns rather than greens.

**Q: What do the mixing quantities mean?**
A: Quantities like "pea-sized" and "dime-sized" are real-world descriptions to help you mix on your palette. You can also select different mix sizes (Test Swatch, Small, Medium, Large) based on how much paint you need.

**Q: Why can't I match a certain color?**
A: Some colors may be outside your palette's gamut. The app will warn you when this happens and suggest paints you could add to extend your range.

---

### Projects & Layers

**Q: What is "fat over lean"?**
A: Fat over lean is a principle where each layer should be more flexible (fattier) than the layer below it. This prevents cracking as the paint dries. The app tracks this automatically and warns you if you're about to break the rule.

**Q: How are drying times calculated?**
A: Drying times are estimated based on the paint's inherent drying speed, the medium used, the layer thickness, and the paint's opacity. These are estimates—actual drying depends on your environment.

---

### iCloud Sync

**Q: Does my data sync between devices?**
A: Yes! Your paint collection, medium collection, recipes, and projects sync via iCloud automatically. Make sure you're signed into the same iCloud account on all devices.

**Q: What if my data isn't syncing?**
A: Check that you're signed into iCloud and that the app has iCloud access in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud. You can also tap "Sync Now" in the app's Settings to force a sync.

---

### Purchases

**Q: How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**
A: Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Your Premium status will be restored if you previously purchased on the same Apple ID.

**Q: Is Premium a subscription?**
A: No! Premium is a one-time purchase of $3.99. You pay once and keep unlimited paints forever, including on future devices using the same Apple ID.
